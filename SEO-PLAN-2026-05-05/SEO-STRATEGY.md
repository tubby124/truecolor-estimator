# True Color Display Printing — SEO Strategy

**Date:** 2026-05-05
**Domain:** truecolorprinting.ca
**Stack:** Next.js 16 / Tailwind v4 / Railway
**GSC baseline (28d):** 70 clicks · 13,094 impressions · 0.53% CTR · pos 32.8 avg
**Indexed pages (public marketing):** 95

## Goal

Move wide-format Roland orders (signs, banners, coroplast, vehicle decals, vehicle wraps, large format) from "trickle" to "consistent weekly inbound." Stickers already convert from social/walk-in/repeat — Google is the unsolved channel for the *higher-AOV Roland line*.

## Core diagnosis

The site is **broad but shallow**. 95 public pages indexed; only 7 of them have ever earned a click in 28 days. Authority is diluted: every niche page competes against itself for the same internal links instead of pointing at flagship hubs.

GSC reveals 3 distinct symptom buckets:

| Bucket | Example | Imp | Pos | Fix |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Page-1, broken CTR** | wall-graphics-saskatoon | 186 | 13 | rewrite title/meta with price + speed |
| **Bottom page-1** | banners-saskatoon | 10 | 11.6 | inject content depth + internal link mass |
| **Page 3+, hub starvation** | sign-company-saskatoon | 45 | 30 | rebuild as flagship hub with 25 internal links |

## Strategic pillars

### 1. Hub-and-spoke restructure
Six flagship hubs, every niche page links up to its hub:
- **Sign Company Saskatoon** (parent of every sign niche)
- **Banner Printing Saskatoon** (parent of all banner verticals)
- **Vehicle Graphics Saskatoon** (parent of decals + magnets + wraps + lettering)
- **Coroplast Signs Saskatoon** (parent of lawn signs / for-rent / for-lease / election / event)
- **Wall & Window Graphics Saskatoon** (parent of murals / window perf / decals)
- **Large Format Printing Saskatoon** (parent of trade show / posters / foamboard)

Stickers stays its own track — already converting, separate sales motion.

### 2. CTR weaponization on existing page-1 rankings
7 pages sit at pos 5–15 with 0 clicks. Title formula:
`[Service] Saskatoon | From $X | [Same-Day or 1-3 Day] | True Color`
Meta formula: lead with price floor, end with "Roland UV-print, in-house design, 216 33rd St W."
Mako, SaskatoonSigns.ca, Sharp Auto Trim do NOT show prices in metas. Price + speed = our wedge.

### 3. Lease signage = real estate crossover
"lease signage saskatchewan" pulls 94 imp, pos 8.56, 0 clicks. Build:
- New page `/for-lease-signs-saskatoon` — coroplast, post-mounted, common sizes 18×24 / 24×36 / 4×8 sheets
- Inject "for lease / for rent / lease signage" copy into existing /property-management-signs-saskatoon
- Cross-link from realtor brand (brevo-realtor) outbound campaigns

### 4. Vehicle wraps decision gate
Sharp Auto Trim (40-yr brand) + 306 Motoring own full wraps. **Decision required before building**:
- **A.** Build full wraps as a service → /vehicle-wraps-saskatoon hub + install partner schema
- **B.** Stay decals/lettering-only → triple down on /vehicle-decals + /vehicle-lettering + /fleet-graphics where Sharp's old-school site loses on UX

### 5. GBP push (Roland services only)
"Business is open at time of search" is now a top-5 local ranking factor (Whitespark 2026). Add:
- 6 Roland-service GBP product posts (one per hub)
- Weekly photo of Roland-print job in progress
- Q&A populated for "do you do coroplast / for lease signs / vehicle wraps?"
- Service-area = Saskatoon + secondary cities (Regina/Moose Jaw/PA/Yorkton — already have city pages)

## Quality gates
