## Their content gaps (our wedge)

1. **No transparent prices in titles or metas** — Mako, SaskatoonSigns, Sharp all hide prices behind quote forms. True Color's "From $X" approach in metas wins 30-50% more clicks at same position.
2. **No same-day messaging** — only Print Linkage even mentions turnaround. "Same-day rush available" in title is open territory.
3. **No real-estate crossover** — nobody owns "lease signage saskatchewan" content despite 94 monthly impressions. Bonus: True Color has the realtor brand to drive demand into supply.
4. **Generic city pages** — competitors mostly target Saskatoon only. True Color has Regina/Moose Jaw/PA/Yorkton/Lloyd/Swift/Estevan pages; rank threats are minimal in those markets.
5. **No GBP product posts cadence** — SaskatoonSigns' GBP is static; weekly Roland job photos + product posts will out-pace them in 90 days.

## Where competitors win and we shouldn't fight them head-on

- **"sign company saskatoon" #1 spot** — owned by saskatoonsigns.ca exact-match domain. Realistic ceiling for True Color: position 3-5 in 6 months. Don't burn months chasing #1.
- **"vehicle wraps saskatoon" full wrap install** — Sharp Auto Trim has the install bay + 40-yr brand. Decision: partner-and-refer OR build install capability. SEO alone won't beat them.
- **"cheap coroplast saskatoon"** — Print Linkage $20 floor. Don't compete on price; compete on speed + design service.
