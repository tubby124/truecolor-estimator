9. Saskatoon Chamber of Commerce

🔗 saskatoonchamber.com/membership
Paid (~$350–$650/yr) High-authority local backlink Best for local credibility

📌 Chamber of Commerce — is it worth it?

  1. Yes — the Saskatoon Chamber member directory is a high-authority local citation (.com domain with strong local trust)
  2. Membership also gives networking access + a Saskatchewan business directory listing
  3. Cost: approximately $350–$650/year depending on membership tier
  4. Call (306) 244-2151 or email chamber@saskatoonchamber.com to ask about the "Small Business" tier
  5. The directory backlink alone is worth ~3–4 months of organic citation building

Bonus: 3 more quick wins after the 9 above

  • Better Business Bureau (bbb.org): Free Canadian listing. Adds trust signal. Search for the business first — may already have a stub.
  • Hotfrog Canada (hotfrog.ca): Free, ~5 min. Aggregated by BrightLocal and Moz Local. Good for citation coverage.
  • Cylex Canada (ca.cylex.ca): Free. Another aggregator that feeds smaller local data networks. Takes 2 min.
