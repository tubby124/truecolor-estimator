# SEO Standards — True Color

## Page Pattern
Component: IndustryPage | Min 400 words description | 8 FAQs minimum
Metadata: title under 60 chars | description 140–155 chars with price + location
Priority: 0.95 tier-1 | 0.90 new local | 0.85 seasonal | 0.80 secondary

## Content Rules
- Price in first paragraph (e.g. "from $8.25/sqft")
- "Saskatchewan" or "Saskatoon" in first paragraph
- "In-house Roland UV printer" mentioned at least once
- "Same-day rush" with "+$40 flat"
- In-house designer mentioned ($35 flat)
- Internal links to 2+ related pages
- NEVER invent prices — verify against data/PRICING_QUICK_REFERENCE.md
