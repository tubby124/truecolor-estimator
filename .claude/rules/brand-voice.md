# Brand Voice — True Color Display Printing

## Tone
Direct, locally-grounded, price-transparent. No fluff. Real specs, real prices, real turnaround times.

## Forbidden phrases
Never use: "competitive pricing", "affordable", "high quality", "best in class", "fast turnaround"
Use instead: specific numbers — "$8.25/sqft", "1–3 business days", "+$40 same-day rush"

## Mandatory in every piece of content
- Price in first paragraph (e.g. "from $8.25/sqft" or "starting at $45 for 250 cards")
- "Saskatchewan" or "Saskatoon" in first 100 words
- "In-house Roland UV printer" — at least once
- "Same-day rush" with "+$40 flat" — NEVER present same-day as free
- In-house designer: "$35 flat, same-day proof" — at least once
- Internal links to 2+ related pages with descriptive anchor text

## Price source of truth
NEVER invent prices. Always verify against `data/PRICING_QUICK_REFERENCE.md`.
Key anchors: Coroplast $8/sqft | Banners $8.25/sqft | ACP $13/sqft | Design $35 | Rush +$40
